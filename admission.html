REQUIREMENTS

  • NSO Birth Certificate
  • High School or College Diploma
  • For High School graduates: Form 137

PROCEDURE

  • Complete the Student Application Form and Pre-Enrollment Agreement. This form can be found at the Admission Desk
  • Present your completed Student Application Form to the Registration Desk for system encoding.
  • Pay the Behavioral and English Assessment fee amounting to Php 500 at the Cashier
  • Proceed to Computer Lab for your admissions exam and interview.
  • Present the results of your exam and interview to the Admission Desk Officer for your Medical Examination Endorsement. All applicants must complete a medical examination on or before the date specified on the endorsement.
  • Submit your admission requirements to the Admission Desk. See the complete list of requirements below.
  • Pick up and complete a Registration Form at the Admission Desk.
  • Submit your completed Registration Form to the Registration Desk Officer and pay the Training Fee at the Cashier. Ensure a copy of your Registration Form at the Registration Desk after payment.
